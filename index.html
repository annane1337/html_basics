Easy Basic Pancakes

Homemade pancakes are a treat, but they are surprisingly simple to whip up. Our easy pancake recipe uses ingredients you likely have on hand, like all-purpose flour, baking powder, sugar, butter, and egg. Start with our basic recipe, or try one of our variations using buttermilk or yogurt. Either way, you'll have a fluffy stack ready to eat in less than half an hour.   • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
